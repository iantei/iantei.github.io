01 About

I'm a software engineer with a Master's in Computer Science from Arizona State University, and hands-on experience spanning machine learning pipelines, embedded systems programming, and cloud-native infrastructure. My background moves across the stack — from low-latency audio engines on medical ultrasound devices in C++/Swift, to fuzzing production DNS infrastructure at Yahoo, to building ML-driven transportation research tools at NREL.

I care about systems that are reliable under real-world load and code that a team can actually maintain. More recently, I've built a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) application for energy policy research and contributed to open-source projects including RouteE Compass and nanvix, and I'm working toward integrating an AI assistant directly into this site.
